VOOOCAB

VOOOCAB 是一款由AI驱动的英语单词本生成工具。可以帮你快速筛选单词，并通过大语言模型（LLM）为这些单词生成个性化的学习材料，最后导出为可打印的 PDF 文档。 I. 注意: ① 本应用需要你自行准备兼容的大语言模型 API Key 进行内容生成。 ② 单词的直译注释(翻译), 采用极小字体（3pt），打印后只有靠近才能看清，旨在引导你体验，猜测词义，而非被直接告知。 II. 单词筛选：① 你可以使用多种方式对单词进行筛选和排序，高效地剔除已掌握的词汇，从而精准整理出你真正需要学习的新词。 ② 用颜色来表示你对每个单词的熟悉度：黑色=不认识, 灰色=略懂, 白色=很熟。 III. 个性化学习材料： ① 在配置 AI (大语言模型 API) 后，应用可以为选定的单词自动生成音标、释义、翻译、同义词对比以及应用场景。 ② 可以自定义部分提示词，比如让AI在什么样的世界环境下用什么样的叙述风格来叙述，以获取个性化的学习材料。 ③ 双重质检机制：你可以选择是否启用 ‘质检功能’，可通过额外的 AI 请求对部分已经生成的内容进行质量校验，对‘不符合你要求’的内容进行‘标记’或‘重新生成’。 (审查准确率取决于模型种类与提示词工程的优化程度。) ④ 两种模式：Type-A：文字量较少。Type-B：文字量较多，侧重于单词的基础描述，语境联想，同义词对比。Type-B 同时支持你导入自己的 ‘本地图片文件库’ 来为每个单词配一张图(建议每张小于150kb) ，如果你没有图片库则会自动留出手绘区域。 IV. 排版与导出： ① 支持将生成的文本自动排版进 A5、A6 等尺寸的 .PDF 文件。 ② 提供 '多排版' 以及 '不同装定方式' 的选项。 ③ 为了方便你装订成册，你可以在导出 .pdf 前根据 页数，册数，预估打印后的 ’实际每册厚度’。 ④ 可以选择导出'.dsv'文件（类似.csv），而非.pdf 。 VOOOCAB is an AI-driven English vocabulary book generator. It helps you quickly filter words and uses Large Language Models (LLMs) to generate personalized learning materials, and eventually exporting them as printable PDF documents. I. Note: ① This app requires you to provide your own compatible LLM API Key for content generation. ② Direct translations are printed in a tiny font (3pt), visible only up close, designed to encourage you to guess the meaning rather than being told directly. II. Word Filtering:① You can filter and sort words in multiple ways to efficiently remove known vocabulary, precisely organizing the new words you truly need to learn. ② Use colors to indicate familiarity: Black = Unknown, Gray = Somewhat known, White = Well known. III. Personalized Materials: ① After configuring the AI (LLM API), the app automatically generates phonetics, definitions, translations, synonym comparisons, and usage scenarios. ② You can customize some of the prompts, such as setting the world context and narrative style, to get personalized materials. ③ Content Review: You can enable the 'Content Review' feature to verify generated content via additional AI requests, marking or regenerating content that doesn't meet your standards. ④ Two Modes: Type-A (less text) and Type-B (more text, focusing on descriptions, context, senarios, and synonyms). Type-B supports importing your own 'local image library' (recommended <150kb per image); otherwise, a blank space is left for hand-drawing after printing. IV. Layout & Export: ① Supports typesetting into A5, A6, etc., (PDF files). ② Offers 'multiple layouts' and 'different binding methods' (or non-binding mode). ③ To help you print them into booklets, you can preview the 'actual thickness per booklet' based on pages and volumes before exporting. ④ You can choose to export a '.dsv' file (similar to .csv) instead of a .pdf.
